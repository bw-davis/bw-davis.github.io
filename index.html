Projects

High Order Calculator Grammar in C

I did a summer research project for the CIS Department Head at the U of O. The goal was to take a chapter from Kernighan and Pike's The Unix Programming Environment that covers grammar and reformat the code. A lot has changed since the book was written in 1984 and there hasn't been any updates to the book. Hence, the code in it's current form wouldn't compile or run properly. The language created is called high order calculator but it in fact is fully functional, statically allocated, call by value language. Here is a link to the github repository. The YACC grammar is in hoc.y and all of the other files I created to hold the supporting functions required for the language to operate.


Student Athlete Mental Health

Three friends and myself competed in the Hackathon 2016 competition and won in the category of Hack Harassment. We made an iOS app that for student athletes that would track their mood, level of activity, diet, sleep, etc. via a questionnaire. They would take the survey every day and it would show them trends in their daily routine that equate to their overall mood being better. The goal is to allow the athlete to be more in tune with their emotional well being and be able to create healthy habits. Here is a link to the github repository.


Marine Corps Career

I joined the Marines in 2004 as a Boeing CH-46E helicopter crewchief. As a crewchief I was responsible for performing pre-flight inspections on the aircraft to ensure that they are safe to fly. Then during the flight mission, I would man the weapons systems, clear the aircraft for left and right turns, perform in-flight inspections, as well as load and unload all of the cargo or passengers we took on during the flight. While I wasn't flying I would perform routine maintenance on the helicopters that weren't flying that day. I had to be intimately knowledgeable about the innerworkings of all the helicopter's systems in order to properly troubleshoot issues.


As I gained in experience I also gained qualifications; such as, Night Systems Instructor, Weapons Instructor, Plane Captain, Collateral Duty Inspector and Quality Assurance Inspector. I was meritoriously promoted to the ranks of lance corporal, corporal and sergeant and was promoted above all of my peers to the rank of staff sergeant within 6 years. That is the rank I held until I was honorably discharged in 2013.
